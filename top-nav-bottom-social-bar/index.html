Top Nav + Bottom Social Bar

This fully responsive navigation pair is built with HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript. On small screens, the top menu features a hidden menu that extends from the left to cover the viewport. The social links are visible as a fixed bar at the bottom of the screen. On larger screens, the social links are visible in the top nav and the bottom social bar is hidden.
